Our Mission

"Grove Heritage Academy exists to provide students with a Christ-centered education focused on academic excellence, leadership, integrity, and spiritual growth. Our mission is to prepare young men and women to thrive in both faith and purpose."

"For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord, plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future."
Jeremiah 29:11
Why Grove Heritage Academy

An Education Rooted in Faith and Excellence

We believe every student deserves an education that honors God and challenges their mind, one that actually prepares them for college, work, and whatever comes next.

Christ-Centered Learning

Every course is taught from a biblical worldview. We don't simply add Bible class. Faith is woven into every subject, every discussion, and every assignment.

College-Preparatory Academics

Our rigorous curriculum prepares students for college admissions and beyond. Graduates enter university confident, capable, and grounded in their faith.

Flexible Homeschool Program

Learn at your own pace, on your own schedule. Our program gives families the flexibility to build their school day around their lives, without sacrificing academic rigor or structure.

What We Offer

What's Included in Our Program

01
Curriculum

Grades 9–12 Courses

Comprehensive high school courses in English, Mathematics, Science, History, Bible Studies, and electives, aligned with college preparatory standards.

02
Records

Transcript Management

Official high school transcripts maintained and available for college applications, transfer requests, and records verification whenever you need them.

03
Guidance

College Preparation

SAT/ACT guidance, college application support, recommendation letters, and academic counseling to help students reach their goals with confidence.

04
Diplomas

Graduation Pathways

Standard and College Preparatory diploma tracks with clear graduation requirements. We celebrate every graduate's achievement and God-given potential.

05
Community

Family Partnership

We believe education is a partnership between school and family. Parents are involved, informed, and supported every step of the way.

06
Spiritual

Biblical Worldview

Our integrated approach to faith and learning helps students develop a coherent biblical worldview they can carry into college, career, and life.

Getting Started

How It Works

Enrolling is straightforward. Here's how families get started with Grove Heritage Academy.

1

Apply Online

Complete our enrollment application in about 15 to 20 minutes. We'll respond within 2 to 3 business days.

2

Choose Your Courses

Work with our academic advisor to select the right courses for your student's grade level and goals.

3

Set Up & Start

Access your student portal, receive materials, and begin learning on a schedule that works for your family.

4

Graduate with Confidence

Earn an accredited diploma with official transcripts, ready for college and life beyond the classroom.
