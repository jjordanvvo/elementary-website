Why Heritage Christian Academy

An Education Rooted in Faith and Excellence

We believe your child deserves an education that honors God, challenges their mind, and prepares them for a life of meaning.

Christ-Centered Learning

Every course is taught from a biblical worldview. We don't simply add Bible class — faith is woven into every subject, every discussion, and every assignment.

College-Preparatory Academics

Our rigorous curriculum prepares students for college admissions and beyond. Graduates enter university confident, capable, and grounded in their faith.

Flexible Homeschool Program

Learn at your own pace, on your own schedule. Our program gives families the freedom to customize their educational journey while maintaining academic excellence.

What We Offer

Everything Your Student Needs to Thrive

01

Grades 9–12 Curriculum

Comprehensive high school courses in English, Mathematics, Science, History, Bible Studies, and electives — all aligned with college preparatory standards.

02

Transcript Management

Official high school transcripts maintained and available for college applications, transfer requests, and records verification whenever you need them.

03

College Preparation

SAT/ACT guidance, college application support, recommendation letters, and academic counseling to help students reach their goals with confidence.

04

Graduation Pathways

Clear graduation requirements with Standard and College Preparatory diploma tracks. We celebrate every graduate's achievement and God-given potential.
