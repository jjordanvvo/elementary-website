Official Records

Transcript & Records Requests

Official transcripts are mailed or emailed directly to colleges, scholarship programs, or other recipients. Please allow 5–7 business days for processing. Current students may request up to 2 free transcripts per year; additional copies are $5 each.

Before You Submit

  • Have your student ID or full legal name and date of birth ready
  • Know the exact mailing address or email for the recipient institution
  • Alumni (graduated more than 2 years ago) should note their graduation year
  • Unofficial transcripts for personal use are available in the Student Portal

Legal Information

Homeschool Legal Information

Heritage Christian Academy is a private homeschool support program. Families enrolled in our program are still considered homeschoolers under their state's laws.

Each state has different requirements for homeschooling, and it is the family's responsibility to understand and comply with their state's regulations.

Common state requirements may include:

  • –Filing a notice of intent to homeschool with your local school district
  • –Maintaining attendance records (typically 170–180 days per year)
  • –Administering standardized testing at certain grade levels
  • –Keeping a portfolio of student work
  • –Submitting annual evaluations

Heritage Christian Academy provides the academic structure, official transcripts, and documentation that many states require. However, we strongly recommend that every family review their own state's homeschool laws.

Recommended Resources

  • –Homeschool Legal Defense Association (HSLDA)
  • –State-by-State Homeschool Laws

If you have questions about how Heritage Christian Academy fits within your state's requirements, contact our admissions team.
