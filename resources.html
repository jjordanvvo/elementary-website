Official Records

Transcript & Records Requests

Official transcripts are mailed or emailed directly to colleges, scholarship programs, or other recipients. Please allow 5–7 business days for processing. Current students may request up to 2 free transcripts per year; additional copies are $5 each.

Before You Submit

  • Have your student ID or full legal name and date of birth ready
  • Know the exact mailing address or email for the recipient institution
  • Alumni (graduated more than 2 years ago) should note their graduation year
  • Unofficial transcripts for personal use are available in the Student Portal
