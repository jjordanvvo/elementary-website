Curriculum Overview

Designed for the Whole Student

Our curriculum is built to provide a comprehensive, college-preparatory education rooted in Christian faith. Every course challenges students to think critically, engage deeply, and grow in wisdom — not just accumulate knowledge.

We use faith-integrated materials across all disciplines. From the science of God's creation to the history of His providence, students graduate with a confident, coherent worldview that prepares them for college classrooms and beyond.

What We Teach

Every subject is taught from a biblical worldview with academic rigor that meets or exceeds standard college preparatory requirements.

Core Required

Bible & Theology

Old Testament Survey, New Testament Survey, Christian Worldview, Apologetics, Ethics & Moral Reasoning. Required every year of enrollment.

Core Required

English & Language Arts

English 9–12, American Literature, British Literature, Creative Writing, Speech & Communication. Strong writing emphasis throughout.

Core Required

Mathematics

Algebra I & II, Geometry, Pre-Calculus, Statistics, Consumer Mathematics. Algebra I through Pre-Calculus required for College Prep diploma.

Core Required

Science

Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Environmental Science, Anatomy & Physiology. At least one laboratory science required for graduation.

Core Required

Social Studies & History

World History, U.S. History, U.S. Government, Economics, Geography. Taught with an awareness of God's providence in history.

Elective

Electives

Spanish I & II, Art, Music Appreciation, Health & Wellness, Introduction to Business, Computer Science. Students choose based on interests and goals.

College Prep

College Preparation

SAT/ACT Prep, College Application Workshop, Career Exploration, Study Skills. Available for 11th and 12th grade students.

Required

Physical Education

Personal Fitness, Team Sports, Health Education. Students may fulfill PE requirements through organized sports, fitness programs, or structured activity logs.

College Readiness

Graduates Who Are Ready for What Comes Next

Our students don't arrive at college unprepared. Through rigorous coursework, SAT/ACT preparation, and personalized college planning guidance, our graduates are equipped to compete for admission and succeed once they arrive.

We provide academic counseling throughout high school, help students craft compelling essays, and ensure their transcript reflects the depth of their education. Faith doesn't just prepare students for eternity — it prepares them for today.
