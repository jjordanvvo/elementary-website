From Application to Diploma

How It Works

From application to graduation — here's what to expect at Grove Heritage Academy.

Step by Step

From Application to Graduation

1

Apply

Complete our online enrollment application. It takes about 15–20 minutes. You'll need your student's previous school records and some basic medical information.

2

Get Accepted

Our admissions team reviews your application within 3–5 business days. We'll reach out to schedule a phone consultation to discuss your student's goals and needs.

3

Choose Your Courses

Work with your academic advisor to select courses and build a personalized academic plan based on your student's grade level, interests, and diploma track.

4

Set Up Your Account

Receive your enrollment packet with login credentials, course materials access, and your student handbook. Everything you need to get started in one place.

5

Start Learning

Your student begins coursework from home on a flexible schedule. Lessons are self-paced with teacher support available throughout the week.

6

Stay on Track

Regular progress reports keep you informed every four weeks. Your academic advisor monitors progress and reaches out if your student needs extra support.

7

Graduate

Complete all graduation requirements and receive your official Grove Heritage Academy diploma and transcripts. Your records are ready for college applications, scholarship programs, or employment.

Sample School Day

A Day in the Life

There's no single right way to structure your school day. Here's a sample schedule that works for many of our families:

8:00 AMMorning devotional and prayer
8:30 AMBible / Theology
9:30 AMMathematics
10:30 AMBreak (15 minutes)
10:45 AMEnglish / Language Arts
11:45 AMLunch break
12:30 PMScience
1:30 PMSocial Studies or Elective
2:30 PMIndependent reading or project work
3:00 PMDone for the day

This is a suggestion, not a requirement. One of the biggest benefits of Grove Heritage Academy is the flexibility to build a schedule that works for your family. Some students work best in the morning. Others prefer afternoon sessions. As long as the work gets done, we support your family's rhythm.

Getting Started

What You'll Need

No special software or expensive setup required. Here's what every student needs:

Computer or laptop (Chromebook, Mac, or Windows)
Reliable internet connection (5+ Mbps)
Updated web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
Printer (recommended, not required)
Email address for student and parent
Webcam and microphone (for occasional live sessions)
Basic school supplies: notebook, pens, calculator

That's it. No special software, no expensive textbooks, no complicated setup.

Ready to Get Started?

Applications are open year-round. The whole process takes about 15–20 minutes.
