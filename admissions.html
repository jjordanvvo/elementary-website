Admissions & Enrollment

We welcome applications year-round. Starting is simpler than you think, and our team will walk you through every step.

How It Works

Joining Grove Heritage Academy Is Simple

We accept applications on a rolling basis. Here's what the process looks like from your first inquiry to your student's first day.

01

Submit an Inquiry

Fill out our online inquiry form and a member of our admissions team will reach out within 48 hours to answer your questions.

02

Schedule a Consultation

Meet with our team to discuss your student's goals, review placement options, and have all your questions answered personally.

03

Complete the Application

Complete the full enrollment application and submit it along with previous transcripts and academic records. Our team reviews applications promptly.

04

Welcome to the Family

Once accepted, you'll receive a welcome packet with everything you need to get started, including login credentials, course access, and your assigned academic advisor's contact information.
