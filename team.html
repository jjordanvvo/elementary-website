Staff & Faculty

Meet the Grove Heritage Team

Small staff, real people. We know our students by name and make ourselves available to every family.

D
Christopher Hayes
Founder & Director

With a heart for Christian education and years of experience working with homeschool families, Christopher Hayes founded Grove Heritage Academy to give high school students a program that takes both academics and faith seriously. She oversees curriculum planning, admissions, and the overall direction of the school.

A
Academic Advisor
Academic Advisor

Our academic advisor works directly with families to build course plans, track student progress, and help students stay on pace for graduation. Whether your student needs extra support or wants to accelerate, Academic Advisor is your go-to resource.

R
Staff Registrar
Registrar & Records

Responsible for maintaining all student records, processing transcript requests, and managing enrollment documentation. Staff Registrar ensures every student's academic history is accurate and up to date.

T
Bible & Theology Instructor
Lead Instructor — Bible & Theology

Our instructor leads the Bible and Theology courses with the same care and rigor you'd expect from any core academic subject. Students have direct access for questions, mentorship, and academic support throughout the year.

We Know Your Student by Name

Grove Heritage Academy staff are committed to knowing every student by name. If you have questions, don't hesitate to reach out.
